About Calgary HVAC Pros

Founded in 1983 by master technician John Thompson, Calgary HVAC Pros began as a small family-operated business dedicated to providing exceptional heating and cooling services to local homeowners. Over four decades, we've grown into Calgary's most trusted HVAC service provider while maintaining our commitment to personalized service and technical excellence.

Our mission is to ensure every Calgary resident and business has access to reliable, efficient, and affordable HVAC solutions. We believe in transparency, integrity, and building lasting relationships with our clients. Our core values of technical excellence, customer satisfaction, and community service guide every decision we make and every service we provide.

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas of Calgary do you service?

We provide comprehensive HVAC services throughout Calgary and surrounding areas, including Airdrie, Cochrane, Chestermere, and Okotoks. Our service area covers all Calgary communities with no additional travel charges.

What are your emergency response times?

For emergency calls, our average response time is under 60 minutes. We maintain fully-stocked service vehicles and technicians on call 24/7 to handle any HVAC emergency, regardless of time or weather conditions.

Do you offer maintenance plans?

Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance plans starting at $199/year. Our plans include bi-annual inspections, priority service, discounted repairs, and extended warranty coverage. Regular maintenance can extend system life by up to 40%.

What warranties do you offer?

We provide a 100% satisfaction guarantee on all services. New installations come with up to 10-year warranties, and repairs are warranted for 90 days. Extended warranty options are available through our maintenance plans.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit, e-transfer, and cash. Financing options are available for larger installations with rates as low as 0% for qualified customers. All pricing is provided upfront with no hidden fees.
