Common HVAC Problems and Solutions: A Homeowner's Guide

February 18, 2025

From poor airflow and unusual noises to temperature inconsistencies and short cycling, HVAC systems can experience various common issues that affect home comfort. Poor airflow often stems from clogged filters or blocked ductwork, while strange sounds may indicate mechanical problems. Uneven heating or cooling could result from improper sizing or duct leaks, and frequent system cycling might signal thermostat issues or low refrigerant. Regular maintenance, including filter changes and professional inspections, can prevent many problems. While some issues can be resolved through DIY maintenance, electrical problems, persistent noises, or system failures require professional intervention.
